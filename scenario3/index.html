Client Overview:


This client is an eCommerce retailer who sells three products that will make your work life 1000% times better. For confidence, t-shirts. For concentration, coffee mug. For coordination, notebooks. As is common with eCommerce sites, this website has a basic category page, individual product pages, a cart page, and a confirmation page. You can navigate to these pages through the menu, or by clicking to visit the All Products page below. Every page has thorough dataLayers that can be used for event triggers and variable capturing.
